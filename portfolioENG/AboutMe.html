Get to know me!

Hello :)

My name's Mina, and I'm an artist from Spain!

Ever since I was little, I've always been drawn to the magic of fantasy worlds and magical characters. Whether it'd be books, series or videogames, I wouldn't let go of it until I had enjoyed every last drop of what the story had to offer me. Of course, with the desire to experience came the wish of wanting to create my own!

As such, I started drawing from a young age, making up characters and stories in my mind, until I discovered actual people worked behind those videogames that looked so awesome and complicated! And so, it was as if I had found the purpose of my life: I wanna make those too!

And here I am now, creating and learning along the way: 2D, 3D, animation, even programming! My desire to make things new and better only grows, and I don't plan on stopping!
